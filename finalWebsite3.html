My Fav Games






These are some of my favorite games to play on my down time (and sometimes when I should be doing work). These games are either Made very well, Are really fun to play, or have made me laugh.

The Walking Dead

I like this game because it is very story driven and makes the game play very immersive. I usually have to play this game when I have absolutely nothing to do, because it takes me a long time to play levels because of all the things you can do.

Super Smash Bros

I don't think that anyone that i know has said they don't like smash bros. It's one of the most fun fighting games that I have ever played, and it's very easy to pick up(hard to master). I'd like to take this time to hype up Super Smash Brothers Ultimate by linking the official website
